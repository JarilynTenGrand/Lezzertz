Peach and Blueberry Cobbler Recipe

Ingredients:
    Filling:
  • 4 c. peaches, sliced 1/8th to 1/4" thick
  • 3 c. blueberries
  • 1/2 c. granulated sugar
  • 3 teaspoons cornstarch
  • Juice and zest of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
  • Pinch salt
    Topping:
  • 1 1/3 c. all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 c. granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1/2 c. (1 stick) butter, cold
  • 2/3 c. buttermilk
Directions:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°. In a large bowl, toss together peaches, blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, zest, vanilla, cinnamon, and a large pinch of salt. Let sit at least 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, make topping: In another large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add butter and use your hands to cut into flour until pea-sized. Add buttermilk and stir until just combined.
  3. Pour fruit filling and all juices into a 9”-x-13” pan. Drop large spoonfuls of topping all over. Bake until top is golden and peaches can be pierced easily with a knife, about 45 minutes. Let cool 15 minutes before serving.
