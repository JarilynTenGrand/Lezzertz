Boozy S'mores Milkshake Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 8 large scoops (about 2 1/2 c.) chocolate ice cream
  • 1/4 c. Baileys
  • 2 oz. vanilla vodka
  • 1/2 c. marshmallow fluff
  • Crushed graham crackers, for rimming
  • Marshmallow bits, for rimming
  • Chocolate fudge, for rimming
  • Whipped cream, for garnish
  • Chocolate squares, for garnish
  • Graham cracker squares, for garnish
Directions:
  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream, Baileys, vanilla vodka, and marshmallow fluff.
  2. Combine crushed graham crackers and marshmallow bits on a small plate. Rim 2 glasses with chocolate fudge and dip into graham crackers and marshmallows.
  3. Pour milkshake into glasses and top each shake with whipped cream, a chocolate square, and a graham cracker square
